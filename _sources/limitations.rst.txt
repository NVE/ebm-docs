Limitations
============

Model functionality
-------------------

The model does not have functionality on: 

- Electrical vehicles
- Buildings used in power intensive industries
- Local energy production, e.g. solar panels
- Energy prices
- Load profiles
- Cost optimization of heating systems
- Cost of energy efficiency measures
- Single buildings
- Years prior to 2020
