Building categories and building codes

The tables below show the different building categories and building codes (TEK) which are used in the model. The building category table shows what types of buildings are included under the main building category. The building categories are the same as defined in the building code except for “light industry and repairs”. From this category the model only uses the area attributed to storage and warehouses, and the category is then named “storage”.

Building categories and associated building types

Building category

Building types

House

Single houses and terraced houses.

Apartment block

Apartment blocks of two or more storeys, contains the area of multiple apartments.

Kindergarten

Kindergartens and buildings at playgrounds.

Retail

Shopping malls, gas stations, grocery stores, other retail buildings.

Hotel

Hotels, motels, hospits.

Sports

Sport halls, ice rinks, swimming facilities and gyms.

Office

City halls, police stations, fire stations, other offices.

Culture

Museums, libraries, religious buildings, other culture buildings.

School

Primary to highschool.

Nursing home

Nursing homes, rehabilitation facilities and health facilities.

Hospital

Hospitals and doctor offices.

University

Universities and laboratories.

Storage

Warehouses and freezing storage.
